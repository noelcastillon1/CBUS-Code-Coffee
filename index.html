About the venue

The Roosevelt Coffee House / Olentangy Brewing Company is a joint coffee shop and brewhouse off of Route 23 in Lewis Center. The site also features a rotating list of food truck vendors. RSVP here!

Accessibility Accessible entry, seating, and restrooms are available. Indoor acoustics may be an issue for those with auditory sensory disorders, but the patio is relatively quiet. Dogs allowed, but only on the patio (barring service animals).
